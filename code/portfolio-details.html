Opera Hasanaginica

This is about the first original post-war opera production in Bosnia and Herzegovina, which brought together great artists. Composer Asim Horozić composed Hasanaginica to the libretto by Nijaz Alispahić, and in creating this work, conductor Miroslav Homen, director Sulejman Kupusović, the choir and ballet of the National Theater Sarajevo, Sarajevo Philharmonic, and outstanding soloists participated: Krunoslav Cigoj, Margit Tomik, Ivica Čikeš, Ivica Šarić, and Amila Bakšić.
